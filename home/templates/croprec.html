Form Explanation

The ultimate aim is to assist farmers or agricultural professionals in making informed decisions about crop selection, optimizing crop yields, and enhancing agricultural productivity. By leveraging data-driven insights, the system helps users choose crops that are well-suited to the prevailing conditions, thereby increasing the likelihood of successful cultivation and improving overall agricultural outcomes.

In general, the goal is to provide users with recommendations for suitable crops to cultivate based on various environmental factors and soil conditions. By collecting data such as nitrogen, phosphorus, potassium levels in the soil, temperature, humidity, pH, and rainfall, the system aims to analyze these parameters and predict the most appropriate crops for cultivation in that specific environment.

The provided form allows users to input data related to nitrogen, phosphorus, potassium, temperature, humidity, pH, and rainfall. Based on this data, the system predicts the recommended crop for cultivation.

It's essential to input accurate data to obtain reliable recommendations. The ranges for acceptable values are as follows:

  • Nitrogen: 0 to 1000 kg/ha
  • Phosphorus: 0 to 1000 kg/ha
  • Potassium: 0 to 1000 kg/ha
  • Temperature: -50°C to 50°C
  • Humidity: 0% to 100%
  • pH: 0 to 14
  • Rainfall: 0 to 1000 mm
